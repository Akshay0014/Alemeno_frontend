How It Works

 

  1. How to use the app
    Simple
    1. Click your meal
    2. Let us know finish the meal
    Our game will take care of the rest of the things

  2. What if I miss taking the meal picture?
    This can happen. Your profile section has a simple tool - Add missing meals - to add the meals you missed taking pictures

  3. How can my kid share meals if they are with other parents or guardian
    Your kid’s game progress is linked with your registered phone number and they can continue their gameplay on different devices. You will have to provide OTP to the caretaker or guardian before they can login into your kid’s game.

  4. How to use a nutrition tracker?
    Nutrition tracker is key to ensure your kids get the nutrition they need. Nutrition tracker shows daily, weekly, and monthly progress on nutrition requirements. We advise parents to ensure they make up for nutrients your kid is lagging on. App will time to time come up with recommendations to ensure your kids get balanced nutrition they need
