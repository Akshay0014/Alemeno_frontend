Frequently Asked Questions

Why should you use Alemeno?

If you would like your kids to eat nutritious food every day and make them healthy eaters of tomorrow, you should use Alemeno. We believe early childhood is the best time to inculcate healthy eating habits in kids and our app does just that for you.

 

How does Alemeno help parents and kids?

Many parents have to convince their kids to eat properly. Naturally, parents feel concerned for their kids' health. Alemeno works in a way that parents and kids have a common goal -  eating healthy every day.

 

We use a simple principle: Kids love fun things like music, stories, and game (who doesn't?). And they'd love it if eating healthy could be as cool as playing a game.

 

Our solution gamifies kids' mealtime to make healthy eating a fun gameplay. The gameplay helps kids learn and imbibe the following healthy eating habits : 

  1. eating a variety of healthy food

  2. eating required quantity of food

  3. eating within the desired time 

  4. eating with own hands

 

With Alemeno, kids will be excited to have their daily portions of nutrients.
