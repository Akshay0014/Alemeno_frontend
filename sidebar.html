About Us

We believe in healthy living. Our application works as a tool to help kids eat healthier every day. In our solution, we use behaviour science and gamification to make healthy eating an engaging and fun activity for kids. This also provides a strong leverage for parents to encourage their kids to imbibe healthy eating habits under their supervision.
